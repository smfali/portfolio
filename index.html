FAQ

What types of projects do you take on?

I work on sales pages, landing pages, email sequences, onboarding flows, product descriptions, app microcopy, lead magnets, and video/VSL scripts.

How does your process work?

We start with a short discovery chat or brief, then I research, draft, and refine based on your feedback. You get clear timelines and deliverables from day one.

Can you match my existing brand voice?

Yes. I review your current assets and build a mini voice guide to keep everything aligned.

Do you offer ongoing support or retainers?

For ongoing campaigns, launches, or recurring content, I’m open to retainer-based work with a consistent monthly scope.
